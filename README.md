Employee Attrition Analysis Using Python
Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data using Python to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and provide data-driven insights for improving employee retention.

Objectives
Explore employee attrition patterns.
Identify the main factors affecting employee turnover.
Analyze relationships between employee characteristics and attrition.
Generate business insights and recommendations.

Key Findings
Employees with lower job satisfaction showed higher attrition rates.
Certain job roles experienced significantly higher turnover.
Monthly income and work-life balance had a strong impact on employee retention.
Employees with fewer years at the company were more likely to leave.
Business Recommendations
Improve employee engagement and satisfaction programs.
Review compensation and benefits packages.
Provide career development opportunities.
Focus on employee work-life balance initiatives.
